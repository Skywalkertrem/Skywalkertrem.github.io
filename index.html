My Co-Op Work Experiences

You have reached Luke Tremble's work term report. On this page you will find information on my past work experiences and the projects I undertook while working for these employers.

About my position at OMAFRA

At OMAFRA I worked under the PICS (Planning Information Coordination Services) Unit also known as KMT (Knowledge Management Team). In my role at PICS I was able to use my software engineering background to bring a unique perspective to the problems that need to be solved on a day to day basis in the unit.

Job Responsibilities

Developing Scripts for Common Tasks

The biggest project that I worked on while at OMAFRA was the development of multiple scripts designed to automate or speed up repetitive tasks. I developed multiple scripts that automated the process of navigating websites while doing menial data entry tasks, thus allowing these tasks to be done more efficiently and with less human input.

I also developed and improved multiple VBA scripts that allowed my team to automatically gather and manipulate data from PDF Reports that were delivered to us by clients. Thus eliminating a task that would otherwise take multiple days to complete.

  • Managing the RPB (Rural Programs Branch) Support inbox

    During my time at OMAFRA I was tasked with managing the support inbox for my branch. This inbox received requests from people inside the branch looking for technical advice on the many systems that my unit was tasked with supporting. Additionally this inbox recieved questions from clients outside of OMAFRA who were using the software that we managed and I was tasked with giving them the appropriate advice and helping to fix their problems whenever possible.

  • AODA (Accessibility for Ontarians With Disabilities Act) Compliance

    the AODA is the main piece of legislation in Ontario that ensures companies live up to the accessability standards of the modern day. It was my job at OMAFRA to ensure that all of the documents that my branch put out was held to the standards set out under the AODA.
